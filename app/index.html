Baseball allstar statistics at the end of 19th until the beginning of 20th century using DC.js

In this visualization, I wanted to visualize Lahman's dataset on how many all-star games certain baseball players appeared in over the years. You can find out more information about this data set here . You can click on data to make it change and use reset button to reset to original state. Libraries used: dc.css (v1.7.5), underscore-min.js(v1.8.3), d3.min.js(v3.5.17), crossfilter.js(v1.3.7), dc.js(v1.7.1) Link to github: here
